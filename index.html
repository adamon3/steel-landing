  • Workout templates you save and reuse

02 · Steal the work

Like a workout? Steel it.

See a session from someone at your gym that looks brilliant? One tap copies it as a template. Run it tomorrow morning. You don't have to design every workout from scratch — borrow from people who already nailed it.

  • Copy any public workout in one tap
  • Templates pre-loaded with weights, reps, exercises
  • Edit before you start — adjust to your level
  • Original athlete gets credited automatically
03 · Your gym, ranked

Compete with the people you actually train with.

Forget global leaderboards full of strangers. Steel ranks you against the actual people in your gym — the ones you nod to at the squat rack. Choose your lift, see where you stand. Healthy rivalry, real motivation.

  • Rankings by exercise — bench, squat, deadlift, more
  • Best estimated 1RM or top set, your choice
  • Filter by week, month, or all-time
  • Opt out anytime — privacy-first by default
