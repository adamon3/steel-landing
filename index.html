02 · Steal the work

Like a workout? Steel it.

Tap Steel It on any session. The lifts, weights, and reps are already filled. Run it tomorrow morning, or save it for next week.

  • One tap, full copy — lifts, weights, reps, rest times, the lot
  • Tweak any set before you start, or mid-workout
  • Original athlete credited on the template
  • Steel any session: a friend's, your own past PR, or a stranger's killer leg day
  • Saved templates live in your Start Workout grid, ready to run
  • Works in reverse — see who's Steeled your sessions
03 · Your gym, ranked

Compete with the people you actually train with.

Pick a lift. See where you sit at your gym. Bench, squat, deadlift, anything you do regularly. Close the gap.

  • Ranked by estimated 1RM or top set, your call
  • Filter by this week, this month, or all-time
  • Top three highlighted, your row pinned wherever you sit
  • Opt out any time and you disappear from the list

05 · Yours, on your terms

Public, private, or fully solo.

Three modes, one switch. Public, Private, or Solo. Flip whenever.

  • Public: your lifts in the feed, on leaderboards
  • Private: lifts hidden, feed still visible
  • Solo: Home, Discover, and Gym tabs gone entirely
  • Switch any time. No data lost, no questions asked.

More inside

All the details you'd want.

Calendar streaks
Every session pinned to its date. Spot the weeks you turned up, and the weeks you didn't.
Progress charts
Auto-tracked 1RM, top set, and session volume per lift. See the line go up, or know when to deload.
Templates
Save any workout as a template. Pull it up tomorrow with one tap, weights and reps already in.
Likes & comments
Cheer your mates on a PR. Drop a "good lift". Light social touch, no algorithmic feed nonsense.
Rest timer
Tap a set complete and the timer counts you down to ready. Your phone buzzes when it's go time, so you can pocket it.
Works offline
No signal at the gym in the basement? Log anyway. Sets queue on your phone and sync when you're back online.
